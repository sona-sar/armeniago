ABOUT US

I'm Sona, a computer science major at Glendale Community College. My connection to Armenia and my interest in tech led me to create this website. It was an idea I've had for a while, and thanks to the project assessment at Snap Academy, it's now a reality. The site highlights beautiful spots in Armenia, catering to various interests and tastes. It also keeps you updated on upcoming events. This project is my way of sharing what I love about Armenia with others.
